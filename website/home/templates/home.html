Description

Our web app leverages the concept of arbitrage in the sports betting market to generate a guaranteed return on investment. It aggregates and analyzes betting lines across multiple bookmakers in live time to identify profitable opportunities. But it also aims to serve as a social platform, where users can share their love of sports and personalize their experience.

Motivation

This domain is lucrative and mathematically intriguing, with the sports betting market seeing exponential growth. Our app explores inefficiencies in this market.

While other platforms provide valuable odds aggregation, they often lack quick, real-time arbitrage opportunities. Our app fills this gap with its real-time, data-centric approach, with the added benefit of having historical data as well!
